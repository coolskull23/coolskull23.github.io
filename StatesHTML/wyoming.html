Wyoming Energy Tax Laws

Disclaimer!! Please speak to a local municipal government if considering the following!

Sales and Use Tax

No General Exemptions:Wyoming doesn't have a broad sales and use tax exemption for renewable energy equipment. However, there are a few specific situations where exemptions might be applicable

  • Pollution Control Equipment: Equipment primarily designed for air or water pollution control may be exempt from sales and use taxes.
  • Construction Materials: Some exemptions exist for materials used in the construction of specific facilities, which might potentially apply to renewable energy projects.

Property Tax Considerations

Wind Energy Property Tax (WEPT):Like some states, Wyoming does impose a property tax specifically on wind energy generation facilities.

Mineral Taxes

Production Taxes (PT):Wyoming has significant taxes on the production of mineral resources, including oil, gas, and coal. These can impact the overall economics of energy development in the state.
