Income Tax Incentives/h2>

Income Tax Incentives (ITI):Vermont itself doesn't currently offer state-level income tax credits or deductions for residential renewable energy installations. However, you can often combine these state-level tax breaks with federal incentives, like those available under the Inflation Reduction Act.
