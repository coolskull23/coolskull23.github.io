Property Tax Exemptions

Solar Energy Systems (RSA 72:62): Solar energy systems installed on residential and commercial properties are exempt from property taxes for an initial period of 10 years. This applies to solar thermal, photovoltaic, and other solar equipment.

Wind Energy Systems (RSA 72:64): Wind-powered energy systems used to generate electricity are exempt from property taxes.

Wood Heating Systems (RSA 72:63): Wood heating systems that are the primary source of heat for a residential property are exempt from property taxes.

Utility Taxes

Metering (RSA 362-A): New Hampshire's net metering law allows electricity customers to offset their electricity usage with energy generated from on-site renewable energy sources. This can significantly lower energy bills.

Rebates & Incentives

Saves Residential Programs: Offers various rebates and incentives for energy-efficient home improvements, including upgrades to appliances, insulation, and heating systems
