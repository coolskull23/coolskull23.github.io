Sales and Use Tax Abatement

Renewable Energy Systems (NRS 701A.300-.370): Nevada offers substantial sales and use tax abatements for the purchase and installation of renewable energy systems. This includes systems designed to produce solar, wind, geothermal, biomass, and other renewable energy sources.

Storage Systems (NRS 701A.300-.370): Energy storage systems, which are crucial for balancing the electricity grid when using renewable sources, may also be eligible for sales and use tax abatements.

Property Tax Abatements

Renewable Energy Systems (NRS 701A.300-.370): Similar to the sales & use tax, Nevada offers property tax abatements for qualifying renewable energy systems. These abatements could significantly reduce property taxes for facilities generating clean energy.

Pollution Control Equipment (NRS 361.0685): Equipment designed primarily for pollution control can be eligible for a partial property tax abatement.
