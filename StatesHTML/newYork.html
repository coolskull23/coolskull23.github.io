Sales and Use Tax Exemptions

Residential Solar Energy Systems Equipment (Tax Law § 1115(a)(32)):The purchase and installation of residential solar energy systems are exempt from New York's state and local sales and use taxes.

Property Tax Exemptions

Real Property Tax Law §487:This law allows for a 15-year property tax exemption for the increased value a property gains from installing solar, wind, fuel cell, farm waste energy generation, or certain geothermal energy systems. There are specifics about which systems qualify, so you'll want to check eligibility closely.

Income Tax Credits

Qualified Solar Energy Equipment Credit (Tax Law § 606(g-1)):This credit is available to homeowners who install solar electric generating equipment. It equals 25% of qualified expenditures up to $5,000.

Clean Energy Businesses Investment Tax Credit and Employment Incentive Credit (Tax Law §§ 18, 18-a):This credit, along with a potential job creation incentive, is designed for businesses in specific clean energy sectors.

Green Building Credit (Tax Law § 19):Buildings receiving specific “green building” certifications may be eligible for a tax credit. There are specific standards required to qualify.
