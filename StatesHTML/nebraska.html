Sales and Use Taxes

Energy System Components (Nebraska Administrative Code Title 316, Chapter 3): Sales of "machinery and equipment" used directly in a renewable energy system for electricity generation are exempt from state and local sales and use taxes. This includes solar, wind, geothermal, biomass, and qualifying hydroelectric.

Property Taxes

Renewable Energy Valuation (Neb. Rev. Stat. ยง77-6203 (4)): In general, renewable energy facilities are taxed as industrial real property for valuation purposes. There is also the stipulation that land under a renewable energy system will continue to be valued as if the energy system did not exist (maintaining its prior agricultural value, for example).

Income Taxes

Energy Tax Credit (Neb. Rev. Stat. ยง 77-27,235): Allows qualifying renewable energy producers to earn a production tax credit of $0.00075 per kilowatt-hour of electricity generated by eligible facilities.
