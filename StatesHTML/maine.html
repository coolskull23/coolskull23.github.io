Federal Tax Credits

Residential Clean Energy Credit (formerly known as the ITC or solar investment tax credit): This now provides a 30% tax credit for installation of solar panels, solar water heaters, geothermal heat pumps, and other eligible renewable energy equipment. It's uncapped, meaning you can claim the full credit regardless of the system costs.

Nonbusiness Energy Property Credit (25C): Provides a tax credit of up to 30% for a variety of energy efficiency upgrades such as insulation, energy-efficient windows and doors, heat pumps, and energy audits.

Maine State Incentives

Efficiency Maine Rebates: Efficiency Maine offers various rebates for energy-efficient upgrades to your home. These include:

  • Heat Pumps: Rebates ranging from $400 to $2,400.
  • Heat Pump Water Heaters: Rebates up to $950.
  • Insulation: Rebates up to $4,000 - $8,000

Sales Tax Exemption: Maine offers a sales tax exemption for the purchase and installation of solar energy systems.
