South Dakota Energy Tax Laws

Disclaimer!! Please speak to a local municipal government if considering the following!

Sales and Use Tax

No General Exemption (NGE):South Dakota doesn't have a broad sales and use tax exemption for renewable energy equipment. However, specific situations might have exemptions.

Property Tax Considerations

Wind Energy Tax (WET):Unlike many states, South Dakota does have a property tax on large-scale wind energy facilities. This tax is in addition to regular property taxes.

Other Incentives

Grants and Loans (GAL):The Governor's Office of Economic Development may offer grants or low-interest loans for some renewable energy or energy efficiency projects. Check their website for current opportunities.
