Delaware Energy Tax Laws

Disclaimer!! Please speak to a local municipal government if considering the following!

Sales and Use Tax

Delaware has no sales tax. This means there are no specific sales tax exemptions for energy-related equipment.

Property Tax

Delaware does not currently offer a property tax exemption for solar energy systems or renewable energy equipment

Other Incentives

Green Energy Program: This program, run by the Delaware Sustainable Energy Utility (DESEU), offers rebates for homeowners and businesses installing solar photovoltaic (PV) systems, solar hot water systems, and geothermal systems. The program also has grant opportunities
