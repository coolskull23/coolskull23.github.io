Kansas Energy Tax Laws

Property Tax Exemptions

Renewable Energy Property Tax Exemption (K.S.A. 79-201): This is the big one! Kansas offers a property tax exemption for renewable energy equipment. However, there are two important considerations:

  • Application Timeline: Applications for exemption filed on or before December 31, 2016 provide a lifetime property tax exemption. Applications filed after December 31, 2016 limit the exemption to 10 years.
  • Eligible Technologies: The exemption covers solar, wind, biomass, geothermal, hydropower (with limitations), and landfill gas generation systems.

Sales and Use Tax Exemptions

Waste Heat to Energy Systems (K.S.A. 79-3606): Systems designed to capture waste heat from electricity generation for further electricity production or to produce renewable fuels are exempt from sales and use taxes for 10 years after installation.
