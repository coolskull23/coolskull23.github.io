Franchise Tax Exemption

Renewable Energy Franchise Tax Exemption (Texas Tax Code § 171.107):Businesses primarily engaged in manufacturing, selling, or installing solar or wind-powered energy devices are eligible for an exemption from the Texas franchise tax.

Sales and Use Tax Exemptions

Solar Energy Devices (Texas Tax Code § 151.355):The sale and installation of residential and some commercial solar energy devices are exempt from sales and use taxes.

Water Conservation Equipment (Texas Tax Code § 151.354):Equipment designed specifically for water conservation can be exempt from sales and use taxes.

Property Tax Exemptions

Energy Storage Systems (Texas Tax Code § 11.311):Property tax exemptions may be available depending on the size and use of energy storage systems (batteries, etc.).

Pollution Control Property (Texas Tax Code § 11.31):Equipment that serves a primary purpose of reducing, controlling, or eliminating air, water, or land pollution may be eligible for a property tax exemption.
