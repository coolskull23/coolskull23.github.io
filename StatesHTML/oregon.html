Oregon Energy Tax Laws

Disclaimer!! Please speak to a local municipal government if considering the following!

Residential Incentives

Residential Energy Tax Credit (RETC) (ORS 316.116):Offers a substantial tax credit for Oregon homeowners who install certain renewable energy systems or make energy efficiency improvements to their homes. Eligible systems include solar, geothermal, small wind, and fuel cell energy installations.

Business Incentives

Business Energy Tax Credit (BETC) (ORS 315.304):Offers businesses tax credits for investing in renewable energy projects and making energy efficiency upgrades.

Property Tax Exemptions

Renewable Energy Systems (ORS 307.175, ORS 307.395):Systems designed to generate power from renewable energy sources like solar and wind can qualify for property tax exemptions, depending on the scale and installation type.
