Income Tax Credits

Biodiesel and Renewable Diesel Sales Equipment Tax Credit (NDCC ยง57-38-69):Retailers can claim a corporate income tax credit for 10% of direct costs incurred in adapting or adding equipment to sell biodiesel or renewable diesel. The credit is limited to $50,000 over 5 years.

Additional Incentives

North Dakota Volkswagen Mitigation Trust Funding:North Dakota received funding from the VW Environmental Mitigation Trust, and a portion is allocated to projects such as replacing diesel vehicles with electric or alternative fuel models and installing EV charging infrastructure.
