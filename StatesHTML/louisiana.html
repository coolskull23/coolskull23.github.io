Louisiana Energy Tax Laws

Disclaimer!! Please speak to a local municipal government if considering the following!

Sales and Use Taxes

Solar Energy Systems (La. R.S. 47:305.51): Sales and use taxes are exempted on the sale, use, lease, or rental of specific solar energy systems used for residential purposes.

Property Taxes

Solar Energy Systems Ad Valorem Tax Exemption (La. R.S. 47:1706): Solar electric and solar thermal systems installed on residential property are exempt from ad valorem taxes.

Income Taxes

Solar Energy Systems Tax Credit (La. R.S. 47:6030): Louisiana offers a tax credit for 50% of the first $25,000 of the cost of installing a solar energy system in a home. This credit is in addition to federal tax credits and is refundable.

Wind Energy Systems Tax Credit (La. R.S. 47:6030): Louisiana also has a tax credit for 50% of the first $25,000 of the cost of installing a wind energy system. Similar to the solar credit, it's additional to any federal credits and is refundable.

Jobs Tax Credit (La. R.S. 47:6037): This credit provides tax incentives for companies investing in projects related to renewable resources, energy efficiency, or environmental conservation.
