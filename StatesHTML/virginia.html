Sales and Use Tax Exemptions

Solar Energy Equipment & Installation (Code of Virginia ยง 58.1-638.1):The sale, lease, and installation of qualified solar energy equipment are exempt from the retail sales and use tax.

Income Tax Considerations

Virginia doesn't offer a state income tax credit for renewable energy or energy efficiency improvements (ITC):However, you might still be able to benefit from federal tax credits, which Virginia often mirrors.

Property Tax Considerations

Potential Local Ordinances (PLO):Virginia doesn't have a statewide property tax exemption for renewable energy systems. However, some localities (counties, cities, towns) may offer their own exemptions. Check with your local tax assessor.
