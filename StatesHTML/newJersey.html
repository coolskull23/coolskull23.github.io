Sales and Use Taxes

Solar Energy Systems (N.J.S.A. 54:32B-8.13): The sale and installation of most solar energy systems for residential and commercial use are exempt from New Jersey's sales and use tax

Energy-Effcient Products (N.J.S.A 54:32B-8.42 and Temporary Sales Tax Exemptions): New Jersey sometimes offers temporary sales tax holidays on the purchase of qualified ENERGY STARÂ® appliances and other energy-efficient products. Check the Department of Treasury's website for current promotions.

Property Taxes

Energy Systems (N.J.S.A. 54:4-3.3): Renewable energy systems installed for residential or commercial purposes, including solar, wind, geothermal, and others, are exempt from property taxes for 15 years from installation. This increases the assessed value of your property without incurring an additional tax burden.
