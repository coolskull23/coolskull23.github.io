Property Tax Exemption

Renewable Energy Property Tax Exemption (IC 6-1.1-12): Indiana offers a property tax exemption for a variety of renewable energy systems installed on residential, commercial, industrial, agricultural, and multifamily properties. Exempt systems include:

  • Solar water heating systems
  • Solar space heating systems
  • Geothermal electric systems
  • Solar thermal process heat systems
  • Solar photovoltaic (PV) systems
  • Wind energy systems (all sizes)
  • Hydroelectric systems
  • Geothermal heat pumps
  • Solar pool heating systems

Sales Tax Exemption

Indiana does not have a specific sales tax exemption for equipment used in renewable energy production.
