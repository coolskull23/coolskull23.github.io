Sales and Use Tax Exemptions

Renewable Energy System Machinery and Equipment (RCW 82.08.02565 & 82.12.02565):The sale, installation, and lease of machinery and equipment, as well as labor and services used in constructing qualifying renewable energy systems, are exempt from both sales and use taxes. This includes solar, wind, geothermal, some biomass, and landfill gas generation systems.

Property Tax Exemptions

Alternative Energy Improvements (RCW 84.36.645):In some cases, depending on local regulations, the increased value of a building due to the installation of alternative energy systems may be eligible for property tax exemptions.

Other Incentives

Alternative Energy Improvements (RCW 84.36.645):In some cases, depending on local regulations, the increased value of a building due to the installation of alternative energy systems may be eligible for property tax exemptions.
